ABOUT ME

I'm Andrew, a sophomore majoring in Computer Science at the University of Pittsburgh.
Prior to college, I had not even dreamed of finding myself doing programming in college.
To me, only people who were exceptionally talented at mathematics could undergo such a pursuit.
Later, in my senior year in high school, I competed in a hackathon for my high school.
While it was incredibly difficult to me, I developed a newfound appreciation for programming.
More specifically, looking at how others utilize different techniques to improve performance.
After this, I was interested in seeing how I could contribute to the medical field using programming.
Thus, I enrolled in the Computer Science program here to accomplish my goals for the future.
Currently, I am applying to Computational Biology Graduate Programs around the nation.
